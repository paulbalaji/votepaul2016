Hi folks!

My name is Paul Balaji. After five months in DoCSoc, I have come back with only one goal - to change the Other Position in my society. But my old approach wasn't enough. I had to become someone else. I had to become something else. I had to become the Green Arrow Vice President.

Webmaster 2015-16

I'm a second year Computing student, and despite all the coursework - I still made time for DoCSoc. As the 2015-16 Webmaster I helped log attendance at bar nights, kept people in the know about cool events, and even made a new IC Hack website. If you had a list of people in descending order of dedication, adding me would take O(1) time, every time.

Always at Events

I always think: what's the point of putting on events if you don't go to them? Over the past year, I have gone to almost every single DoCSoc event - not out of obligation, rather because it's a fantastic way to meet more people on your course in a casual environment. With a range of experience in attending events, you can always rely on me to show up.

Fastest Man Alive

You didn't see that coming?

Many people come to me as the first port of call for DoCSoc, and I have always replied rapidly. I know I do not have all the answers, but I will always try and get you one step closer to the solution.

My Manifesto

The Short Version

I care about students very much, and have a year of DoCSoc under my belt. I will change Other Position into Vice President, and lead DoCSoc into a new era of peace and prosperity. Hail Hydra!

The Long Version

The past year has been quite extraordinary for DoCSoc. We are now getting more sponsorship, better events, and awesome interaction from students. Seriously, it's time-consuming and sometimes mind-crushing to put on all these events but at the end of the day – you, the students, are turning up and engaging. That makes everything worthwhile. This is why I wanted to be in DoCSoc last year, and it is the same reason I am running in the elections this year.

Mums and Dads introduction, the Careers Fair, Bar Nights, IC Hack – these are all major events that I helped run. From getting coffee for sponsors to driving towards Nando's to pick up a cheeky order, I have experience in the nitty gritty operations of DoCSoc. This makes me the perfect candidate for the Other Position because I will make it THE Other Position.

My true end game is to turn the Other Position into a Vice President role. This would mark the change in stature of DoCSoc, from a simple departmental society to a whole new beast. The society is growing faster each year, and it's simply not possible for the President to bear the responsibilities him/herself.

Being there last year, I would aid the President in guiding the new committee to pastures new. There is no doubt DoCSoc will be improving next year, so vote for me and let's make it extra special. Getting into office is not just a victory for me – it is a victory for us.
